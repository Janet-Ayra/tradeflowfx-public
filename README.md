## What is TradeFlowFX?

TradeFlowFX is a **native desktop trading journal** that helps traders understand *why* they win and lose — not just whether they do. It's the only journal built around psychology: correlating your emotional state and confidence level directly with your P&L outcomes.

> *"TradeFlowFX is the first journal that actually showed me why I was losing. Turns out I had a 0% win rate every time I traded feeling anxious."*  
> — Early beta tester · Forex & indices trader

## ✨ Features

### 🧠 Psychology-Aware Journaling
Log your emotional state at entry. See exactly which moods build your edge — and which ones destroy it.

```
Confident  →  86% WR  ·  +53.4 pips
Anxious    →  20% WR  ·  +3.8 pips
Greedy     →   0% WR  ·  -27.7 pips
```

### 📋 Pre-Trade Checklist
Your strategy rules displayed before every entry. No more forgetting your plan the moment price moves.

### 🏆 Trade Score Grading
Every trade graded on **process quality**, not just outcome.

| Grade | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| **A** | Followed plan perfectly |
| **B** | Minor deviation |
| **C** | Significant deviation |
| **D** | Broke rules entirely |

### 📅 Visual Calendar Heatmap
See your entire month at a glance — green days, red days, streaks, and drawdowns.

### 📥 Broker Import
Auto-detects CSV formats from MT4, MT5, cTrader, TradingView, Interactive Brokers, and ThinkorSwim.

### 🔒 Private by Default
- Fully offline — your data never leaves your machine
- No account required
- No tracking, no telemetry, no cookies
- No subscription — ever
